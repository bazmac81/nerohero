What is a Nero Hero?

A member of your community that has shown the vales that are important to us at Nero

What are your values?

We have a very simple set of values but they mean a lot. When people join Caffe Nero, we look for honest, caring people that have integrity and are resiliant among many others. During this last year it's people that display this values in what they do that have helped communities through difficult times and we'd like to help you recognise and thank them.

How do I nominate my Hero?

Simply click the link to complete the nomination form and tell us about your community hero.
